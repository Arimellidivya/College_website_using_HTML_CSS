About Divya College of Engineering

A Unique Heritage College

As the founder of our engineering college, I envisioned a place where innovation meets excellence, and where students are empowered to shape the future. Events

Anvena Techno-cultural fest

The Techno-Cultural Fest is a lively event showcasing the talents and cultural diversity of the college community. It features a variety of activities, including competitions, performances, and workshops, fostering creativity and camaraderie among students.

Sports Day

Sports Day is an annual event at the college, showcasing a range of sports and games. It fosters a sense of community, healthy competition, and teamwork among students, making it a memorable and engaging experience for all.

Sankranti Sambaralu

Pongal, a traditional harvest festival, is celebrated with great enthusiasm in college, featuring cultural programs, traditional food, and festive decorations, fostering a sense of community and joy among students and faculty.

Women's Day

International Women's Day, celebrated annually on March 8, honors women's achievements and promotes gender equality worldwide. It's a day to recognize and celebrate the social, economic, cultural, and political achievements of women.
